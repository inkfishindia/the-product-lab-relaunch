## Program Overview

**Objective:** Transform The Product Lab from a broad merchandise storefront into a sharp, differentiated lifestyle brand with stronger product hierarchy, tighter identity, better conversion, and a relaunch campaign that resets market perception.

**Platform:** Commerce.com (Fynd) | **Budget:** Bootstrap (<₹5K/mo per tool) | **Market:** India, mobile-first
