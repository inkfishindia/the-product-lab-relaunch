## How to Use This Document

These are copy-paste-ready templates for every customer touchpoint. All follow brand voice rules:
- Opinionated, never corporate
- Group chat register — like a friend recommending
- Honest, edge on humor
- Short enough for WhatsApp (primary channel)
- Minimal personalization needed

**Instructions for Dan:**
1. Copy the template body
2. Replace bracketed fields like [Customer Name] or [Product Name]
3. Paste into WhatsApp, SMS, or email as appropriate
4. Send immediately (voice is conversational, not polished)

## 1. Order Confirmation — Prepaid

**Trigger:** Customer completes prepaid (UPI/card) purchase

**Channel:** WhatsApp (primary) / Email (backup)

**Body:**

---

Order confirmed. Here's what just happened:

Order #[ORDER_ID]
[Number of items] item(s) | ₹[TOTAL_PRICE]

Your [PRODUCT_NAMES] is packed and heading out tomorrow morning. You'll get tracking by 11am.

Prepaid pays off: saved ₹30 with this order.

Thanks for being here from Day 1.

---

**Notes:**
- Keep it short — this is WhatsApp first
- Include order number for reference
- Price confirmation builds confidence
- The "₹30 savings" line reminds them why prepaid is better

## 2. Order Confirmation — COD

**Trigger:** Customer places cash-on-delivery order

**Channel:** WhatsApp (primary) / Email (backup)

**Body:**

---

Order confirmed. Here's what just happened:

Order #[ORDER_ID]
[Number of items] item(s) | ₹[TOTAL_PRICE]

Your [PRODUCT_NAMES] is packed and heading out tomorrow. Pay the courier when it arrives — exact amount, no change needed.

Tracking link coming by 11am.

---

**Notes:**
- No ₹30 savings line (COD doesn't get prepaid discount)
- Exact amount reminder reduces friction (courier doesn't need to make change)
- Keep tracking message separate (comes next)

---

## 3. Shipping Notification

**Trigger:** Order dispatched from warehouse

**Channel:** WhatsApp (primary) / Email (backup)

**Body:**

---

Out for delivery.

Order #[ORDER_ID]
Tracking: [TRACKING_LINK]
Carrier: [CARRIER_NAME]
AWB: [AWB_NUMBER]

Should land in [DELIVERY_WINDOW] — exact date in the tracking link.

Click the link to see exactly where it is right now.

---

**Notes:**
- Lead with the key info — it's on the way
- Include both link and AWB (some customers prefer one over the other)
- Delivery window is the promise; tracking link is the detail

---

## 4. Delivery Notification

**Trigger:** Carrier confirms delivery / item handed to recipient

**Channel:** WhatsApp

**Body:**

---

Delivered.

Order #[ORDER_ID] landed with [RECIPIENT_NAME].

Got what you ordered? Let us know if anything's off.

(If this wasn't you, tell us immediately.)

---

**Notes:**
- Short and simple — delivery is the good news
- Opens the door for feedback without being pushy
- Addresses the rare case of wrong recipient

## 5. Post-Delivery Thank You & Review Request

**Trigger:** 3 days after delivery confirmed

**Channel:** WhatsApp

**Body:**

---

How is [PRODUCT_NAME]?

No pressure — just want to know if it landed right and if the [PRODUCT_NAME] is everything you expected.

(And if it wasn't, tell us. We'll make it right.)

---

**Notes:**
- Casual tone — not a corporate survey
- Opens door for both positive feedback and complaints
- Assurance that we'll fix issues reduces friction to respond negatively

## 6. Refund Initiated — Prepaid Order

**Trigger:** Refund processing starts (prepaid customer)

**Channel:** WhatsApp / Email

**Body:**

---

Refund approved.

Order #[ORDER_ID]
Refund amount: ₹[REFUND_AMOUNT]
Return received: [DATE]

The ₹[REFUND_AMOUNT] is heading back to your [CARD/UPI] account. Should land in 3–5 business days depending on your bank.

---

**Notes:**
- Be specific about method (card vs. UPI vs. bank transfer)
- Timeline is realistic (5-7 days) — managing expectations reduces follow-up
- No apologies or over-explanation; just facts

---

## 7. Refund Initiated — COD Order

**Trigger:** Refund processing starts (COD customer)

**Channel:** WhatsApp

**Body:**

---

Refund approved.

Order #[ORDER_ID]
Refund amount: ₹[REFUND_AMOUNT]
Return received: [DATE]

Since you paid COD, we're sending the ₹[REFUND_AMOUNT] to your bank account. This takes 5–7 business days once we file it with the bank. You'll get the bank transfer reference once it processes.

---

**Notes:**
- COD refunds are bank transfers (not cash) — set clear expectations
- Timeline is longer than prepaid because bank is involved
- Reference number helps customer track on their end

## 8. Return Requested Confirmation

**Trigger:** Customer initiates return request

**Channel:** WhatsApp / Email

**Body:**

---

Return request confirmed.

Order #[ORDER_ID]
Item(s): [PRODUCT_NAMES]
Refund: ₹[REFUND_AMOUNT] (once we receive & verify)

Here's what happens next:
[IF WE'RE PICKING UP] We'll pick it up from [ADDRESS] by [DATE]. Have it ready to hand over.
[IF PREPAID LABEL] Here's your prepaid return label: [LABEL_LINK]. Drop it at any [CARRIER] location.

Send us a photo of the AWB once it's picked up — helps us track the return.

---

**Notes:**
- Provide clear next steps (pickup or prepaid label)
- Ask for AWB photo to stay on top of return status
- Timeline to refund is "once verified" — not promising instant money back

## 9. Exchange Request Confirmation

**Trigger:** Customer requests exchange for different size/color

**Channel:** WhatsApp

**Body:**

---

Exchange approved.

Order #[ORDER_ID]
Item: [ORIGINAL_PRODUCT]
Exchange for: [NEW_PRODUCT] — [SIZE/COLOR]

New item: ₹[NEW_PRICE] | Original: ₹[ORIGINAL_PRICE]
[IF PRICE DIFFERENCE] We'll [REFUND ₹X / CHARGE ₹X] for the difference.

[NEW_PRODUCT] ships by [DATE]. Send back the original in the prepaid label we're including — should arrive same time.

---

**Notes:**
- Handle price differences transparently
- Two shipments happening (outbound + return) — make sure customer knows
- Timing should sync (new item arrives before return is due)

## 10. Out of Stock Notification

**Trigger:** Ordered product unexpectedly went out of stock

**Channel:** WhatsApp

**Body:**

---

Quick heads up.

The [PRODUCT_NAME] just sold out while your order was processing. What now?

Option 1: We refund you (full ₹[PRICE] back by tomorrow)
Option 2: We ship [SIMILAR_ALTERNATIVE_PRODUCT] at the same price instead
Option 3: We hold your order and ship [PRODUCT_NAME] when it's back in stock (5–7 days)

Your call. Let us know by [TIME].

---

**Notes:**
- Give customer agency (three clear choices)
- Timing is urgent (need decision by specific time) without being pushy
- Similar alternatives prevent losing the sale

---

## 12. COD Payment Reminder

**Trigger:** COD order out for delivery, payment not yet made

**Channel:** WhatsApp (if needed)

**Body:**

---

Your [PRODUCT_NAME] is being delivered today.

When the courier shows up:
- Check the item (open it if you want — the courier can wait)
- Pay exactly ₹[AMOUNT] (they usually have change, but exact is easier)
- Ask for receipt and note the AWB number

Courier will take it from there.

---

**Notes:**
- This is optional — only send if there's a pattern of payment delays
- Removes ambiguity about payment process
- Gives customer confidence to check item before paying

## 13. Issue Resolution — Damaged Item

**Trigger:** Customer reports damage in transit

**Channel:** WhatsApp

**Body:**

---

Got it. Sending a replacement right now.

Order #[ORDER_ID]
Issue: [DAMAGED/DEFECTIVE]
Photo received: [YES]

New [PRODUCT_NAME] ships by [DATE]. Same address as the original order.

The damaged one? Keep it (don't need it back). We'll handle it on our end.

---

**Notes:**
- Take action immediately (don't ask for more details)
- Assurance they don't need to return damaged item (removes friction)
- Timeline for replacement is the real promise

---

## 14. Issue Resolution — Wrong Item Shipped

**Trigger:** Customer received incorrect SKU

**Channel:** WhatsApp

**Body:**

---

That's on us.

Order #[ORDER_ID]
Shipped: [WRONG_PRODUCT]
Should have been: [CORRECT_PRODUCT]

We're sending the right one to you today. Keep the wrong item (our mistake, not your problem).

Correct [PRODUCT_NAME] arrives by [DATE].

---

**Notes:**
- Immediate ownership of mistake
- No return request or hassle for customer
- Ship the right item and move on

## 15. Lost/Delayed Shipment Resolution

**Trigger:** Order missing after 7 days with no movement

**Channel:** WhatsApp / Phone call

**Body:**

---

Your order went missing. Let's fix it.

Order #[ORDER_ID]
Status: Lost in transit (carrier confirmed)

Two options:
Option 1: We reship the same items (arrive [DATE])
Option 2: Full refund (₹[AMOUNT]) back to your original payment method

Your call. Which works better?

---

**Notes:**
- State the problem directly (no dancing around it)
- Two clear solutions; let customer choose
- Timeline for reship or refund is concrete

## 16. Post-Purchase Upsell — Related Product

**Trigger:** Customer bought one item from a collection; similar item exists

**Channel:** WhatsApp (wait 5+ days after delivery)

**Body:**

---

Since you got the [PRODUCT_NAME], thought you'd like this too.

[SIMILAR_PRODUCT_NAME] — same vibe, different message. Same price range.

[LINK]

No pressure. But if it's your style, it's there.

---

**Notes:**
- Only send 5+ days after delivery (not immediately after purchase)
- Frame as "thought of you" not "buy more"
- One product recommendation max; don't spam

## 18. Re-Engagement — Lapsed Customer

**Trigger:** Customer who bought 60+ days ago with no repeat purchase

**Channel:** WhatsApp

**Body:**

---

Missing your energy on our feed.

New [COLLECTION_NAME] just dropped. It's not your old [PRODUCT_NAME], but same chaos, different format.

[LINK]

If you're over us, cool. But if you want back in, it's here.

---

**Notes:**
- Casual tone acknowledges the gap without guilt-tripping
- Suggests what's new (don't assume they want the old thing)
- "If you're over us" gives graceful exit (increases likelihood they'll engage)

## 19. Bulk Order Confirmation

**Trigger:** Corporate/bulk order placed and confirmed

**Channel:** Email / WhatsApp

**Body:**

---

Bulk order confirmed.

Order #[ORDER_ID]
Items: [PRODUCT_NAMES]
Quantity: [TOTAL_UNITS]
Price: ₹[TOTAL]

We're coordinating production with [MAKER_NAME]. Timeline: [PRODUCTION_DAYS] for production, [SHIPPING_DAYS] for delivery = [TOTAL_DAYS] days total.

You'll get updates at [CHECKPOINTS]. Any questions, hit us up.

---

**Notes:**
- Include maker name (transparency + assurance)
- Break down timeline (production vs. shipping) so customer knows what's realistic
- Proactive checkpoint communication reduces anxiety

**Channel:** WhatsApp (to gift purchaser)

Gift order confirmed.

Shipping to: [RECIPIENT_NAME], [RECIPIENT_ADDRESS]
Gift message: "[GIFT_MESSAGE_PREVIEW]"

If recipient details change, tell us by [TIME].

---

**Notes:**

- Reassure that sender identity is hidden
- Clear deadline if changes needed

## Generic Escalation Response

**Trigger:** Issue Dan can't resolve immediately via templates above

**Channel:** WhatsApp

**Body:**

---

Got it. This one needs a closer look.

I'm on it. You'll hear back from me by [TIME/TOMORROW] with a solution.

In the meantime, if you need to reach me urgently, [WHATSAPP_NUMBER].

---

**Notes:**
- Acknowledge immediately (don't go dark)
- Set realistic expectations for response time
- Personal touch (first person, not "the team")
- Provide direct contact for urgency
