## Voice System Applied

**Register map for PDPs:**
- Identity Hook → HEADLINE register (max 8 words, points at the person)
- Why This → CONVERSATION register (1–2 sentences, what it says, why it's collectible/giftable)
- WhatsApp Line → ASIDE register (in parentheses, lowercase, under 15 words, survives forwarding with zero page context)
- Specs → CONVERSATION register (product type + honest format — always last)

**Find. Collect. Gift. mechanic (D-019):** Every Why This and WhatsApp Line is written to make the product feel collectible for yourself or obviously right as a gift. The angle shifts per product — some are self-purchase declarations, some are "this is exactly them" gifts.

**Honesty guardrail on specs (updated 2026-06-13 — Andy):** Materials and construction are now grounded in production data (knowledge/21 + 17): all accessories are UV-printed acrylic; stickers are premium vinyl. No dimensions or materials were fabricated. The only remaining flag is `[print size: confirm per SKU]` (plus `[clasp type]`/`[finish]` on earrings/stickers) where the per-design printed dimension genuinely varies and isn't yet measured. These flags are stripped from the live Medusa descriptions (they don't ship to customers) and kept here as the backfill marker. See "Spec Status" under Flags for Harley. Do not fabricate numbers.

**Banned words:** Quirky, Unique, Fun, Curated, Explore, Premium, Vibrant, Everyday, Special, Amazing, Welcome to, Oops, Delight, Journey, Passion, Innovative, Elevate, Thoughtfully designed — none appear in this copy.
