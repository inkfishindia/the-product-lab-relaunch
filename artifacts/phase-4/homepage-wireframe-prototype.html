Homepage section 3

Trust before proof exists

Since reviews will be light at launch, the homepage has to substitute design confidence, artist framing, logistics clarity, and returns language for missing social proof.

Signal 1 Artist-made

Use artist attribution and original-design framing as a trust device, not just a nice-to-have detail.

Signal 2 7-day returns

Clear reassurance for first-time buyers. This lowers fear without softening the brand voice.

Signal 3 2-4 day shipping

Operational clarity matters because the gifting audience is buying against a date, not abstract desire.

Homepage section 4

Gifting made easier

The gifting route should feel like a personality navigator, not like a backup catalog page.

Route 1 For the sarcastic one

Sharp voice products grouped by recipient personality. This is where gift shopping becomes fast and specific.

Route 2 For the impossible-to-shop-for

Bundle-first route for the buyer who wants appropriateness over endless option-scrolling.

Route 3 Under ₹499, still specific

Price clarity should sit beside personality clarity so the page respects both budget and taste.
