## The Day 30 Review — What to Pull

Before the Day 30 review meeting (Eli + Nik report to Harley, Harley escalates to Dan if needed), pull the following data:

### From GA4
- Total sessions (Day 1–30)
- Conversion rate (transactions / sessions)
- AOV (average order value)
- Top 10 products by revenue
- Top 10 products by units sold
- Add-to-cart rate
- Cart abandonment rate
- Traffic sources breakdown (organic social, direct, referral)
- Mobile vs desktop split (expect 80%+ mobile)
- Geographic breakdown (which cities)

### From Fynd / Razorpay
- Total orders
- COD vs prepaid ratio
- Return/refund rate
- Average items per order

### From Klaviyo/Mailchimp
- Welcome flow open rates (email 1, 2, 3)
- Welcome flow click rates
- Abandoned cart recovery rate
- Post-purchase flow performance
- Subscriber growth rate (Day 1 vs Day 30)

### From MSG91/Gupshup
- WhatsApp broadcast open rate
- WhatsApp CTR
- Opt-out rate

### From Microsoft Clarity
- Heatmaps on homepage, PDP, cart
- Session recordings (watch 20 minimum — especially rage clicks and dead clicks)
- Scroll depth on product pages

### From Instagram
- Reach and impressions (posts vs Reels)
- Profile visits from content
- Link-in-bio clicks
- Story views and swipe-up rate
- Top-performing posts (save, share, comment rate)
- Follower growth Day 1–30

## Go/No-Go Framework at Day 30

Nik presents this to Harley. Harley escalates to Dan.

| Scenario | Decision |
|----------|----------|
| CR >2%, AOV >₹350, 50+ orders | Green: activate Phase 5.5 (first paid ads, Drop 2 planning) |
| CR 1-2%, AOV ₹250-350, 20-50 orders | Amber: optimize funnel first, delay paid spend, schedule Drop 2 |
| CR <1%, AOV <₹250, <20 orders | Red: diagnose root cause before any spend. Escalate to Dan. |

The diagnosis questions for Red:
- Was it a traffic problem (too few sessions) or a conversion problem (traffic but no purchase)?
- Which page has the highest drop-off rate in GA4?
- What do Clarity session recordings show on the product page?
- Are there product trust issues (no reviews, no social proof)?
- Was the content calendar fully executed? If not, what was skipped?
