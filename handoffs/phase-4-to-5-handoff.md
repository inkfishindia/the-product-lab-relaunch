## Voice Rules Reminder for All Phase 5 Agents

These apply to every word published externally.

1. Have an opinion or stay quiet.
2. Talk like the smartest person in the group chat, not like a brand.
3. The humor has an edge. Keep the edge.
4. Name the person. Not the product.
5. Short enough to forward on WhatsApp.

**Banned words (never use):** quirky, unique (standalone), fun, curated, explore, premium, vibrant, everyday, special, amazing, welcome to, oops, delight, journey, passion, innovative, elevate, thoughtfully designed.
