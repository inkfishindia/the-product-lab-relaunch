Scale Gate 1: PMF Validation (Day 30)

🎯 Go/No-Go Criteria

GO if: >1.5% conversion rate, ₹350+ AOV, >500 subscribers, >20 UGC posts from gifting, zero P0 outages
NO-GO if: Below thresholds. Extend Phase 5 optimisation. Fix underlying issues before investing in scale.

First human hire: Store + Packaging Associate (P1). ₹15-25K/mo. Handles daily store ops, packaging, walk-in customers.
Systems: Daily order tracker, inventory reconciliation, weekly cash flow check.

📊 Decision: Medusa

Re-evaluate Medusa + Supabase migration (D-022). 3 leads (QA/Ops/Analytics) must independently recommend. If yes, begin spike; if no, stay on Fynd for 6 more months.

Scale Gate 2: Repeat & Retention (Day 90)

🎯 Go/No-Go Criteria

GO if: >15% repeat purchase rate, >2.5% conversion rate, ₹400+ AOV, positive unit economics at 2x ad spend, 5K+ Instagram followers
NO-GO if: Repeat rate <10%. Focus on retention mechanics (opinion wall, WhatsApp broadcast, collect sets, gifting).

New Functions Required at Scale

🆕 Paid Advertising

Meta + Instagram ads. Google Shopping. Retargeting. Audience building. Budget allocation. Phase 5 is organic-only; paid ads begin at Phase 6 go/no-go. Requires Growth Marketing Manager (P2). Budget: ₹30-50K/month pilot → ₹2-3L/month at scale.

🆕 Omnichannel Expansion

Myntra/Amazon marketplace listing. Quick-commerce (Zepto/Blinkit) for sticker accessories. Franchise retail for Tier 2 expansion. Requires Full-Stack Developer to manage integrations + catalog sync. 70% revenue from online marketplaces (Nasher Miles benchmark).

🆕 International Shipping

UAE (large Indian diaspora), Singapore, US. Shiprocket international rates. Pricing adjustment for international (₹149-999 INR = ~$2-12 USD). Requires Ops lead to configure zones + Fulfillment Coord for international packing.

🆕 B2B Corporate Gifting

Leverage existing enterprise clients (Meta, Diageo, Coca-Cola, Amazon). Corporate gifting vertical on website. Bulk order UI, custom branding option. Requires Product Manager + Relationship Manager.

🆕 Artist Platform

Structured artist onboarding. Tiered revenue share (60/40 → 70/30). Drop calendar (monthly). Co-marketing with artist audience. Requires Artist Relations Manager (P3) + full workflow from sign to launch.

🆕 Data Infrastructure

GA4 → BigQuery pipeline. Customer 360 view. Cohort analysis. LTV modelling. A/B testing framework. Automated reporting dashboard. Requires Data Analyst (P3 freelance). Budget: ₹30-50K/mo.
