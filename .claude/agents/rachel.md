## Indian Market Context

- Instagram Reels is the dominant discovery channel for Indian youth. Prioritize Reels over all other formats.
- YouTube Shorts is growing fast and serves a slightly different audience. Do not treat as Reels reposts.
- WhatsApp Status is underused by brands but high-potential for authentic, ephemeral content.
- Indian meme culture is fast, regional, and reference-heavy. Participate only when authentic to the brand.
- Festival-driven content moments: Diwali, Holi, Raksha Bandhan, Independence Day, cricket events.
- ShareChat and Moj for tier 2/3 reach if expansion warrants.
- Social proof (tagged posts, story mentions) carries more weight in Indian purchase decisions than Western markets.
- Instagram Shopping and product tagging are growing in Indian commerce.
