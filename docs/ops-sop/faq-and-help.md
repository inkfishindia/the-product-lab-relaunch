## 1. Shipping & Delivery

### Q: How long does shipping take?

A: 2–7 days depending on your city. We use multiple carriers — exact timing appears in your tracking email when the order ships. North India: 2–3 days. South India: 4–5 days. Northeast/remote areas: 5–7 days. If you haven't received tracking 24 hours after ordering, tell us — something went wrong.

### Q: Can I choose which courier company ships my order?

A: No, we pick based on speed and cost to your area. But you can see which carrier is handling it in your tracking notification — the AWB number gets you real-time updates.

### Q: Does my order ship on weekends?

A: No. Orders placed Friday evening through Sunday dispatch Monday morning. This doesn't change your delivery date — we just don't ship on weekends.

### Q: I'm in a remote area. Can you still ship to me?

A: Depends on the area. At checkout, if we can't ship to your postcode, the form will tell you. If it says we ship there but you're unsure, ask — we might be able to make it work via a neighboring larger town and local pickup.

### Q: What if my item doesn't arrive?

A: Tell us immediately with your order number. We'll file a missing shipment claim with the carrier. If your order was prepaid, we'll refund or reship once the carrier confirms it's lost (usually 7–10 days). If it was COD and not delivered, no money left your account — we'll sort the carrier and reship at no extra cost.

### Q: Can I track my order?

A: Yes. You'll get a WhatsApp message and/or email with the tracking link the day it ships. Open the link to see real-time location and estimated delivery. You can also ask us anytime with your order number.

### Q: Why hasn't my tracking updated in 2 days?

A: Carriers don't always update in real time. Doesn't mean it's lost. But if it's been 5+ days with zero movement, tell us — we'll check with the carrier and either reship or refund depending on what you prefer.

### Q: Free shipping, really?

A: Yes, on orders ₹499+. Below ₹499, shipping is ₹30–60 depending on distance. COD has a minimum of ₹299 (free shipping doesn't apply to COD — you pay the carrier fee when you pay the order amount on delivery).

### Q: I live outside India. Can you ship internationally?

A: Not yet. Only within India. But if you're interested, send us a message — we're tracking demand for international expansion.

## 2. Payments & Order Placement

### Q: Which payment methods do you accept?

A: UPI, debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Amazon Pay), and cash on delivery (COD). You pick at checkout — whichever works for you.

### Q: Is it safe to enter my card details?

A: Yes. We use Razorpay for payments — they're PCI-DSS certified and handle payments for thousands of Indian businesses. Your card never touches our servers; Razorpay handles it all encrypted. Same security as Amazon or Flipkart.

### Q: Can I use someone else's card to pay?

A: Yes, as long as they approve. The card holder won't see your address or order details — just that a payment went through. Just make sure the billing address matches the card's registered address or it might fail.

### Q: What if my payment fails?

A: No money is debited on a failed payment. Try again immediately. If it keeps failing, try a different payment method. If the issue persists, contact your bank — sometimes banks block transactions they think are fraud.

### Q: I got charged twice. What do I do?

A: Tell us immediately with your order number and transaction ID. We'll check if it's a duplicate charge or two separate orders. If it's a duplicate, we'll refund the extra charge within 2–3 business days. Your bank might also reverse it on their end.

### Q: Is there a discount for first-time orders?

A: No. But if you prepay (use UPI/card instead of COD), save ₹30 off your order. Orders ₹499+ get free shipping, which is the real discount.

### Q: Why is COD only available above ₹299?

A: COD has real costs for the carrier — cash handling, verification, return-to-origin if you refuse delivery. Below ₹299, those costs eat most of our margin. We need a minimum order size to make it work.

### Q: I want to place an order but I'm not sure if it'll work for me. Can you confirm before I pay?

A: Sure, message us with what you want and your address. We'll confirm if we can deliver to you and what the shipping cost will be. Then you can proceed with confidence.

### Q: Can I place an order by WhatsApp instead of the website?

A: Not yet. Website only for now. It takes 2 minutes — you'll be faster ordering online than typing out your address to us.

## 3. Returns & Exchanges

### Q: What's your return policy?

A: 30 days from delivery. Item must be unworn, unused, tags on, packaging intact. Original condition = original price refund. We'll handle the return logistics at no cost to you.

### Q: What if the item arrived damaged?

A: Tell us immediately with a photo. If it's defective (enamel chipped, print damaged, stitching failed), we'll replace it or refund at no cost — including return shipping.

### Q: Can I exchange for a different size/color?

A: If we have it in stock, yes. We'll reship the new one and accept the return of the old one (same 30-day unworn condition applies). If it's a different price, we adjust — you pay the difference or we refund it.

### Q: What if I return an item — when do I get my refund?

A: Prepaid orders: 5–7 business days after we receive and verify the return. COD orders: Same timeline, but the refund comes as a bank transfer (not cash) since we never had your cash.

### Q: Do I have to pay return shipping?

A: No. We'll either pick up from you (if available in your city) or send you a prepaid label. Either way, you don't pay.

### Q: Can I return an item that was a gift?

A: Yes, same policy applies. You don't need the original receipt — we'll look up the order by your email or phone. Refund goes back to whoever paid for it.

### Q: What if I opened the packaging but didn't wear the item?

A: Still returnable. As long as the item itself is unworn and has no defects, the packaging state doesn't matter. We care that the product is sellable again, not that the box looks perfect.

### Q: I want to return an item but it's been 35 days. Can I still return it?

A: No. 30 days is firm — that's the India ecommerce standard. If it's a genuine defect though (item broke after 30 days), tell us — we might work with you even after the window.

### Q: Do you accept returns for items from Design Yatra (the original store)?

A: Not through this site. Design Yatra returns go through their process. This site is separate — you'd need to have bought from us (theproductlab.in) to return through us.

## 4. Product Information

### Q: What material is [product] made from?

A: Every product has full specs on its page — material, dimensions, care instructions. If the page doesn't specify, message us with the product name and we'll tell you.

### Q: Are the materials sustainable/ethically sourced?

A: We work with Indian makers and production partners who we know directly. Not certified organic/Fair Trade (yet), but everything is made by named makers, not faceless factories. We're committed to transparency — if you want to know who made something, ask.

### Q: How do I care for [product]?

A: Specs on the product page include care instructions. General rule: no dishwasher, no extreme heat, keep enamel away from sharp edges. Each product page has specific guidance. If it's not there, ask.

### Q: Is the ink/enamel/print food-safe?

A: No enameled keychains or stickers go near food. The ink used in prints (for card stickers, pins, magnets) is not food-safe. This is a decorative products brand, not dishware.

### Q: Can I use the card sticker on my actual debit/credit card?

A: Yes, if the sticker is designed for card-size products. It'll stay on through normal use (tapping, swiping, swiping at ATMs). But don't fold the card or the sticker might crack at the fold. Also, some banks' cards have textured surfaces — test the fit before you fully commit it to your favorite card.

### Q: Can the keychain survive getting wet?

A: The enamel and metal are both water-resistant. The clasp is sturdy. It's not waterproof (don't leave it underwater), but rain and splashes are fine.

### Q: What's the difference between the half-card and full-card sticker?

A: Half-card covers the front of a debit/credit card. Full-card is larger and works on the back of a card, on other surfaces, or as a standalone sticker. Full-card is ₹249, half-card is ₹199. Same design, different application.

### Q: Do you have products for [specific person/interest]?

A: Check the collections page to browse by vibe. If you don't see what you want, message us — we might have something not yet listed, or we can note what you're looking for for future drops.

### Q: Are these products available anywhere else?

A: No. These are only sold here. We don't sell on Amazon, Flipkart, or other marketplaces. Direct from us only.

### Q: Can I buy in bulk?

A: Yes. Orders of 50+ units, message us for a quote. Bulk pricing depends on the product, quantity, and customization you need. Typical timeline: 3–4 weeks for production + 1 week for delivery.

### Q: Can we customize products for our company?

A: Depends. Simple text changes (names, company logo) we might be able to do. Complex custom designs, it gets harder and more expensive. Message us with specifics and we'll tell you if it's doable and what it costs.

### Q: We want to gift these to our employees. What's the process?

A: Order through the website (you can place a bulk order and use the note field to tag employee names/departments). Or message us for a custom bulk quote with your employee list and we'll handle shipping to each person directly if that's easier.

### Q: What's the lead time for 200 units?

A: 3–4 weeks for production + 1 week for shipment. Total 4–5 weeks. We'll need designs locked and deposit paid within the first week, then we'll give you a more exact date.

### Q: Do you offer wholesale to retailers?

A: Not right now. We're D2C only (selling direct to customers). But if you're a retail partner interested in stocking us, message — we can discuss down the line.

## 6. Tracking & Order Status

### Q: How do I know if my order shipped?

A: You'll get a WhatsApp message and/or email with "Your order is on the way" + a tracking link. If you don't get that message 24 hours after ordering, something's wrong — tell us.

### Q: What's an AWB number?

A: It's the courier's tracking code — like a serial number for your shipment. It's in your tracking message. Paste it into the carrier's website to see exactly where your order is right now.

### Q: I lost my tracking link. How do I check the status?

A: Message us with your order number (or email address you used to order). We'll send the tracking link again. Or if your order already arrived, we'll confirm that.

### Q: My order status says "in transit" but it's been 10 days. What's going on?

A: Carriers don't always update their systems instantly. If it's been 5+ days with zero movement AND it's past your promised delivery window, tell us — we'll claim it missing and reship or refund.

### Q: Can I change my delivery address after I've ordered?

A: Only if the order hasn't shipped yet (same day usually). Message immediately with your new address and we'll try to update it. If it's already packed, too late — you'll need to return it and reorder, or arrange a redirect with the courier (sometimes possible).

### Q: I ordered prepaid but I see COD charges on delivery. What's this?

A: Error on our end. The order should be prepaid (fully paid) — you shouldn't be asked for money at delivery. Tell us immediately and we'll sort it with the courier and refund any amount you paid in cash.

## 7. Technical Issues

### Q: The website isn't loading for me. What do I do?

A: Try these in order: (1) Refresh the page. (2) Clear your browser cache. (3) Try a different browser. (4) Try mobile data instead of WiFi. If none of that works, message us with what you see and we'll debug it.

### Q: The payment page is stuck/loading forever.

A: Wait 30 seconds. If still stuck, refresh the page and try again. Do NOT click "pay" multiple times — that can cause duplicate charges. If you get stuck again, try a different payment method or contact your bank to see if they're blocking the payment.

### Q: I see an error message at checkout. What does it mean?

A: Screenshot the error and message it to us. We'll tell you what went wrong and how to fix it. Common ones: invalid ZIP code, address too long, payment method not compatible with your card.

### Q: The app crashed while I was checking out. Did my order go through?

A: Check your email or ask us by phone/WhatsApp with your email address. We can see all attempted orders. If the order went through, you'll have a confirmation. If the payment failed, you won't be charged.

### Q: Why won't my postcode work at checkout?

A: Some carriers don't service certain postcodes. The form will tell you if we can't ship to you. If the form rejects your postcode but you think we should be able to ship there, message us with your full address and we'll check manually.

### Q: I'm having trouble uploading a gift message. What's the issue?

A: Character limit is 140 words. If your message is longer, shorten it. If that's not the issue, clear your browser cache and try again. If it still doesn't work, message us and we'll add the message manually.

### Q: Can you include a gift message?

### Q: Can I gift to someone without them knowing my identity?

### Q: What if I buy a gift but then want to return it after the recipient gets it?

### Q: Can I send a gift to multiple people at once?
