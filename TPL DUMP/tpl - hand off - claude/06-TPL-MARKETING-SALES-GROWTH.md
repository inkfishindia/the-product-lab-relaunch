- Income: $10,000+ annually

- 78% value comfort/convenience over price
- Growing from 40M to 88M by 2028

**Primary: Gen Z (18-25)**
- 76% follow influencers
- 62% willing to pay premium for personalization
- 54% willing to pay 10% more for eco-friendly
- Social media-driven purchasing

**Secondary: Upper-Middle-Class Households**
- Expected to generate 85% of e-retail GMV by 2028
- Average willingness to pay 30.7% premium for personalized items
