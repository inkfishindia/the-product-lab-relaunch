## YD-TPL Merger / Equity Swap

### Deal Timeline & Evolution

**Phase 1: Initial Proposal (Equity Stake Model)**
- YD Revenue: ₹4 Cr → Valuation: ₹12 Cr (3x)
- TPL Revenue: ₹32L → Valuation: ₹96L (3x)
- Dan's Equity Stake in YD: 8% (₹96L ÷ ₹12 Cr)
- Structure: TPL absorbed completely; Dan on YD payroll with salary + commission + annual profit distribution

**Phase 2: Additional Stake Purchase**
- Dan to buy additional 6% → total 14% of YD
- 65% cash + 35% goodwill/effort credit against reduced salary
- Salary: ₹1L/month (vs ₹2.67L potential) for first year
- Commission: 1% on monthly sales
- Effort credit: ₹25.2L over 18 months
- Cash payment: ₹46.8L structured as ₹2.6L/month

**Phase 3: Email Negotiation (Updated Revenue)**
- YD: ₹4.2 Cr revenue → ₹12.6 Cr valuation (3x)
- TPL: ₹48L revenue → ₹1.44 Cr valuation (later cited as ₹1Cr → ₹3 Cr)
- 15% shareholding pool discussed
- Dan's position: TPL brings 8% of the 15% → his contribution is 7%
- YD's counter: 8% should be computed against 100% of company, not 15%
- **Compromise offer**: Payout reduced from ₹76L to ₹39L
  - 5% upfront at signing (₹1.95L) — non-negotiable
  - 95% over 36 months (₹37.05L)
  - Shares transfer proportionally with payments

**Phase 4: Updated Valuation (FY25 Data)**
- YD FY25 Revenue: ₹4.89 Cr → Valuation: ₹14.67 Cr
- TPL FY25 Revenue: ₹0.31 Cr → Valuation: ₹9.22 Cr (disputed — likely using different revenue base)
- YD is 15.77x larger than TPL
- Growth rates: YD 191.1% YoY; TPL 63.1% YoY
- Recommended: 25% cross-investment (YD gets 25% of TPL; TPL gets 15.7% of YD)

### Key Questions Flagged
1. TPL valuation cited as both ₹96L and ₹9.22 Cr in different analyses — significant discrepancy
2. Revenue figures inconsistent across conversations (₹32L, ₹48L, ₹1Cr)
3. Mathematical dispute in email about how to calculate 8% vs 15% share
