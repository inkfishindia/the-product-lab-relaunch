## Dual Engine Model

TPL operates as two integrated engines:

### The Creative Lab
A physical and conceptual space where artists experiment with formats, materials, techniques, and customization — from 12-color screen printing to UV finishes. Functions like an incubator-meets-maker-lab, giving artists hands-on access to the tools and processes of product creation. Where collaborations begin, ideas get prototyped, and new formats are born.

### The Cultural Marketplace
A curated online destination where collections, capsules, and product lines — born from these experiments — come to life. Not just a shop; it's a gallery of usable art. Whether it's wearable art, functional merchandise, or statement accessories, every product is designed to fuse creativity + utility.
