## Artist Revenue Share Model (Tiered)

| Tier | Revenue Share | Criteria |
|---|---|---|
| New Artists (0-3 months) | 40% of net revenue | Onboarding phase |
| Proven/Established Artists (3-12 months) | 50% of net revenue | Consistent performance |
| Star Artists (12+ months) | 60% of net revenue | Proven track record |
| Exclusive Artists | +10% on applicable tier | Exclusivity agreement signed |
| **Lab Fee** | 15-25% based on final price | Applied across all tiers |

### Net Revenue Calculation
```
Gross Sales = Quantity × Selling Price
Platform Fees = Sales × Platform Fee %
Processing Fees = Sales × Payment Processing %
Net Revenue = Gross Sales - Platform Fees - Processing Fees

Artist Share = Net Revenue × Tier Percentage
```

### Deduction Framework
- Returns/Refunds: Deducted from Gross Sales
- Chargebacks: Artist share protected for first instance
- Quality Issues: Case-by-case review

## Artist Onboarding Process (4-Week Pipeline)

### Week 1: Discovery & Outreach
- Portfolio review
- Market potential assessment
- Social media audience analysis
- Brand alignment check
- Introduction meeting

### Week 2: Evaluation
- Style versatility examples
- Production timeline assessment
- Revenue expectations discussion
- **Evaluation Matrix**: Artistic alignment (40%), Market appeal (30%), Production capability (20%), Personal brand fit (10%)
- **Scoring**: 7+ out of 10 for acceptance

### Week 3: Contracting & Orientation
- Artist agreement review
- Revenue split explanation (tiered structure)
- Intellectual property rights
- Exclusivity terms (if applicable)
- Termination clauses
- **Onboarding Package**: Brand guidelines, product specifications, design templates, creative brief formats, marketing support overview
- **Orientation Session**: Platform walkthrough, payment system, communication protocol

### Week 4: First Drop Planning
- Market trend analysis
- Customer feedback integration
- Seasonal relevance check
- Feasibility assessment
- Production timeline creation
- Initial concept submission → Feedback/iterations (max 3 rounds) → Final approval → Production files → QC checklist
