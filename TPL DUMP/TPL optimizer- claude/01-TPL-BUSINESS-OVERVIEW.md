## Business Model

TPL operates as a **product manufacturing and multi-channel distribution** business with the following characteristics:

- **Own-brand manufacturing** — TPL manages its own factories, materials, and production schedules
- **Artist collaboration model** — TPL works with artists for product collections/collaborations (referenced in Airtable analysis)
- **JIT (Just-In-Time) inventory** — The business runs a JIT inventory system to minimize stock holding costs
- **Multi-channel sales** — Three distinct order systems: Online (WooCommerce), Offline (B2B/in-person), Retail (store)
- **Unified order management** — Master Order Management table provides cross-channel visibility
