## REVENUE SHARING TIERS

| Tier | Duration | Commission Share | Support Level | Min Commitment |
|------|----------|-----------------|---------------|----------------|
| **Emerging Artist** | 0-3 months | 40% of net profit | Foundational | 2 designs/month |
| **Established Artist** | 3-12 months | 50% of net profit | Enhanced | 3-4 designs/month |
| **Signature Artist** | 12+ months | 60% of net profit | Premium | 4-6 designs/month |

### Revenue Calculation Formula
```
Net Profit = Selling Price - (Base Product Cost + Lab Fee + Platform Fee)
Artist Share = Net Profit × Revenue Share Percentage
Lab Fee = 15-25% of selling price (based on product complexity)
Platform Fee = 5-8% of transaction value
```

## ARTIST ONBOARDING PIPELINE

### Phase 1: Discovery & Vetting (7 days)
1. Portfolio review — 10-15 best digital artworks in high resolution
2. Artistic quality assessment and market fit analysis
3. Cultural relevance and creative collaboration potential evaluation
4. Social media presence and audience engagement metrics review
5. Brief artistic statement and creative goals review

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- Artistic quality and technical skill level
- Market appeal and commercial viability
- Brand alignment and cultural relevance
- Growth potential and professional commitment

### Phase 2: Partnership Development (14 days)
1. Creative partnership agreement and revenue terms
2. Platform training and workflow integration
3. Brand guidelines and quality standards orientation
4. Community introduction and peer networking
5. Brand storytelling and artist spotlight development

### Phase 3: Launch Preparation (30 days)
1. Design consultation and market positioning
2. Product selection and customization options
3. Marketing content creation and storytelling
4. Launch strategy and promotional planning

**Time to First Drop Target:** 30-45 days from signing

## ARTIST CREATIVE WORKFLOW

### Design Submission Process
1. **Concept Development** — Market research, creative brief, concept sketches, TPL consultation
2. **Design Creation** — High-resolution artwork, multiple format preparation (print-ready)
3. **Quality Review** — Color variations, product mockups, brand alignment check
4. **Production** — Digital-to-physical conversion via Ink Fish facility
5. **Launch** — Drop coordination, marketing content, social media push

### IP & Creative Rights
- Creative control remains with the artist
- Clear usage rights and attribution standards
- Artist credit and recognition in all materials
- Royalty protection and ongoing revenue streams
- Legal support and contract negotiation assistance
