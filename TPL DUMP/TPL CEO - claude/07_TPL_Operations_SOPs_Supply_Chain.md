## OPERATIONS EXECUTIVE DAILY WORKFLOW (SIMPLIFIED)

### Morning Block (9-11 AM)
1. Check orders (e-com on ShipRocket, retail on TPL APP, B2B on WhatsApp)
2. Check pending/open orders, update delays and issues
3. Make today's priority list
4. Allocate specific products with quantities to assembly team
5. Retail staff check store inventory → request assembly team to make needed products
6. Call factories to check what's ready

### Midday Block (11 AM-1 PM)
1. Answer customer messages quickly
2. Follow up on pending client approvals
3. Check stock levels and note what's running low

### Afternoon Block (1-5 PM)
1. Process dispatches and update tracking
2. Handle any escalations or quality issues
3. Coordinate with design group for client requirements

### End of Day
1. Send daily summary to Dan and Hajira
2. Prep tomorrow's production list
3. Update all systems

### Weekly Rhythm
- **Monday:** Plan the week (simple goals)
- **Wednesday:** Check progress (fix problems)
- **Friday:** Wrap up and plan next week

### Monthly Focus
- Week 1: Customer focus (make customers happy)
- Week 2: Operations focus (make everything run better)
- Week 3: Team focus (help team grow)
- Week 4: Business focus (plan for growth)
