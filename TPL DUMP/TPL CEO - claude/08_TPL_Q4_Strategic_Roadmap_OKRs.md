## DECISION FRAMEWORKS

### RAPID Method (Daily Decisions)
- **R** — Recommend: System provides data-driven recommendation
- **A** — Agree: Dan reviews and approves/modifies
- **P** — Perform: Team executes with clear accountability
- **I** — Input: Stakeholders provide feedback and data
- **D** — Decide: Final decision recorded in system

### Decision Velocity Framework
| Decision Size | Criteria | Response Time |
|--------------|----------|---------------|
| Quick | ≤₹10k AND ≤24hr impact | Immediate |
| Medium | ₹10-50k AND ≤1-week impact | 2-hour analysis |
| Strategic | ≥₹50k AND ≥1-month impact | Full strategic review |

### Morning Power Hour (9:00 AM Automated Briefing)
- Yesterday's performance vs. targets
- Today's priority actions (top 3)
- Critical issues requiring attention
- Cash flow status and projections
- Key meetings and deadlines
