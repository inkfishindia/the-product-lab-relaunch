## MISSION & VISION

**Mission:** To build India's most dynamic creative lab by empowering artists, co-creating with brands, and making drop culture accessible to everyday life.

**Vision:** To become India's first independent design-label platform — part marketplace, part agency, part cultural archive — producing art-led products, incubating creative talent, and influencing the visual language of Indian youth culture.
