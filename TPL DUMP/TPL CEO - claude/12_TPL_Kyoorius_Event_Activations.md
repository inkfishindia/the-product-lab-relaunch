## THE PROGRESSIVE BADGE EXPERIENCE

### 3-Day Layered Stamp System
| Day | Layer | Effect |
|-----|-------|--------|
| Day 1 | "Kyoorius" | Outline of the elephant logo |
| Day 2 | "Stay" | Middle layer building the design |
| Day 3 | "Always" | Completion of the full elephant |

**Execution:** Screen-print effect layered stamps (not UV printing)
- Creates FOMO and drives daily attendance
- Becomes a collectible (scarcity + creativity)
- Innovative crowd control and verification tagging
- Each day's stamp transforms the design through color layering

## ACTIVATION COMPONENTS

### 1. Design-to-Product Workshop Series
- Live demonstrations of digital → physical transformation
- 3 daily workshops: "AI to Print," "Typography to Product," "Digital to Physical"
- Pre-registered participants only (exclusivity)
- 3 invited artists to host workshops (from TPL's connected pool)
- Free for any student that pre-signs up from ticket holders

### 2. Mini-Manufacturing Theater
- Live DTF / screen printing demonstration
- On-demand Kyoorius-branded products
- Options: DTF/screen for tees and totes, print-and-cut stationery, mini laser cut, plotter cut, gold foiled notebooks, luggage tags
- Eco-friendly product options available
- Interactive experience showing design industry innovation

### 3. Artist Showcase Pavilion
- Gallery-come-playground experience
- Artist residency showcase
- Content creation opportunities
- Requires space on premise
