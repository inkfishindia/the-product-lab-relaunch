| Punky Pins | — | Retro aesthetic, specialty retail distribution |

### TPL Market Gaps to Exploit
1. **Indian cultural integration** — No boutique brand does this
2. **B2B creative services** — Most focus purely on D2C
3. **Experience-driven commerce** — Limited physical experience integration
4. **Artist development programs** — Most have basic partnerships only
5. **Technology-enhanced creativity** — Minimal AR/digital integration
