## ASSEMBLY STATION SPECIFICATIONS

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|

| **Team Size** | 2 people on a desk (Person A + Person B) |

## PERSON A (LEFT SIDE) — SORTING & PREPARATION

### Responsibilities
1. Retrieve incoming bin from mini table
2. Sort components at left station
3. Prep materials in center tool area
4. Hand off to Person B
5. Update sorting log
6. Return to incoming station

### Component Organization (6-Compartment Tray System)

**Tray 1 — Accessories Components:**
- Split Rings (various sizes for keychains)
- Round Magnets (standard thickness)
- Rubber Backings (small, medium)
- Magnetic Backings (lapel pin standard)

**Tray 2 — Adhesives & Tools:**
- Fevi Quick Extra Thick Glue (primary)
- Precision applicators
- Glue cleanup materials
- Backup adhesive options

**Tray 3 — Quality Control Items:**
- Magnet strength tester
- Precision measuring tools
- Surface cleaning materials
- Quality reference samples

## PERSON B (RIGHT SIDE) — ASSEMBLY & PACKING

### Assembly Zones by Complexity

**NO ASSEMBLY ZONE:**
| Product | Action | Time |
|---------|--------|------|
| Laptop Stickers | Quality check only | 30 sec |
| 3D Stickers | Inspection + gum drop prep | 45 sec |
| Enamel Mugs | Final QC only | 1 min |

**SIMPLE ASSEMBLY ZONE:**
| Product | Action | Tools | Time |
|---------|--------|-------|------|
| Keychains | Pliers + split rings | Pliers | 1–2 min |
| Coaster Sets | Pairing + arrangement | None | 1–2 min |

**MODERATE ASSEMBLY ZONE:**
| Product | Action | Tools | Time |
|---------|--------|-------|------|
| Lapel Pins | Glue + backing attachment | Fevi Quick | 2–3 min |
| Fridge Magnets | Glue + magnet backing | Fevi Quick | 3–4 min |

### Packing Materials Organization

**Standard Protection:**
- Seal King pouches
- Basic protective sleeves
- Standard TPL branded cards
- Regular shipping materials

**Premium Protection:**
- Gift boxes (wood products)
- Bubble wrap (multiple sizes)
- Premium tissue paper
- Branded premium packaging

**Special Handling:**
- Non-magnetic boxes (for magnets)
- Flat rigid mailers (stickers)
- Shape protection inserts
- Fragile handling materials
