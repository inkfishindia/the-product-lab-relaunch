# The Product Lab (TPL) — Instagram Strategy & Campaign Plan

> **Source:** Past conversation dated June 12, 2025 — "Instagram Marketing Strategy Blueprint"

## Strategic Assessment

### Brand Strengths to Leverage
- Artist-first community approach with strong creative DNA
- Unique product range with customization capabilities
- Visual distinctiveness (CMYK palette, grid layouts, sticker/zine aesthetic)
- Multi-vertical business model spanning D2C, B2B, and physical retail

### Growth Opportunities Identified
- Building a stronger community-driven content ecosystem
- Enhancing product storytelling through visual merchandising
- Leveraging artist collaborations for content amplification
- Developing a more systematic approach to content pillars

## Content Architecture — 4 Pillars

| Pillar | Focus | Content Types |
|---|---|---|
| 🎨 **CREATIVE CHAOS** | Product-focused | Visually striking designs, BTS of production/customization, "What's in the Box?!" unboxing |
| 👥 **ARTIST UNIVERSE** | Community/creator | Collaborator spotlights & takeovers, artist process videos, royalty success stories |
| 🛍️ **MERCH CULTURE** | Commerce/lifestyle | Product drops, limited editions, urban context shots, "Spot in the Wild" customer content |
| ✨ **LAB EXPERIMENTS** | Innovation/events | Workshop content, experimental product tests/prototypes, pop-up & El Patio activations |

---

## Content Format Strategy

| Format | Weight | Content Types |
|---|---|---|
| **Reels** | 60% (Primary) | Product process videos (15-30s), artist spotlight mini-docs, trend participation with TPL twist, quick product demos |
| **Carousel Posts** | 25% (Secondary) | Multi-product showcases, zine-style stacked content, educational/storytelling |
| **Stories / Other** | 15% | Polls, stickers, interactive Q&A, behind-the-scenes |

## Campaign Framework: Card Design / Payment Personality Campaign

### Campaign Concept
A campaign centered around customizable card designs with a "Payment Personality" angle targeting college/Gen-Z audiences.

### Implementation Timeline (8-Week Plan)

| Week | Phase | Monday | Wednesday | Friday |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | Discover | Launch teaser content | Activate first wave of influencers | Release "Payment Personality" quiz |
| **2** | Discover/Define | Share initial UGC and reactions | Introduce campaign concept | Full campaign reveal with hero Reel |
| **3-4** | Define/Design | Launch "Card Personality" series | Feature first collection of designs | Announce design submission contest |
| **5-6** | Design/Deploy | Share application tutorials | Feature college-specific designs | Launch full collection |
| **7-8** | Deploy/Scale | Announce contest winners | Launch exclusive designs | Feature top-performing styles |

### KPIs & Metrics
- UGC volume
- Profile visits to website clicks conversion
- Comment sentiment analysis
- Product tag click-through rate

### Optimization Schedule
- Week 1: Analyze initial content performance
- Week 3: Refine messaging based on engagement
- Week 5: Scale top-performing design categories
- Week 7: Adjust college targeting based on response
