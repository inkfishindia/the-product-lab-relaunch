# The Product Lab (TPL) — Target Audience & Customer Segments

## Ideal Customer Segments

| # | Segment | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Independent Artists & Designers** | Creators looking to monetize their art via merch without handling production, logistics, or inventory |
| 2 | **D2C Customizers & Hobbyists** | Individuals who want to personalize products for gifting, celebrations, or self-expression |
| 3 | **Influencers, Community Leaders & Celebrities** | Niche creators and online personalities seeking ready-to-launch merch with design and fulfillment support |
| 4 | **Startups & SMEs** | Growing businesses that need branded merchandise for marketing, team culture, and events |
| 5 | **Brands & Event Organizers** | Companies running campaigns, festivals, or activations that require custom, seasonal, or limited-run merch |
| 6 | **Retail Hosts (Cafes, Gyms, Spaces)** | High-footfall locations looking to host TPL's micro-retail kiosks for revenue-share, plug-and-play sales |
| 7 | **Agencies & Creative Studios** | Design and marketing firms needing white-label POD infrastructure to serve multiple clients at scale |
| 8 | **Students & Gen-Z Shoppers** | Design-forward, hype-driven consumers attracted to limited edition drops, collabs, and custom items |
