# The Product Lab (TPL) — Brand Creative & Visual Design Guide

## Brand Overview (Creative Guide)

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Positioning** | Artist-powered merch, made to spark joy, bold expression, and street-level curiosity |
| **Mission** | Empower creators and fans with expressive, collectible, and creatively experimental merchandise |
| **Tone of Voice** | Bold, playful, culturally sharp, creative-first |
| **Keywords** | Creative chaos, street-smart, expressive, high-vibe, bold-print, modular, merch-first |

---

## Core Visual Style — Design DNA

- Retro-inspired meets modern modular
- Mystery box energy
- Bold grid layouts
- Sticker pack / zine page influence
- Graphic-obsessed, merch-native

---

## Color Palette (CMYK Inspired)

| Color | Hex Code | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Cyan Blue | `#00B5E2` | Primary accent |
| Magenta Pink | `#EC008C` | Primary accent |
| Yellow | `#FFD100` | Primary accent |
| Black | `#000000` | Text, outlines, contrast |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, text |
| Beige / Off-White | `#FDF7EC` / `#F4F1E7` | Neutral base |

---

## Typography

| Role | Font Options |
|---|---|
| **Headline** | FUTURA / Bebas Neue (all caps, condensed) |
| **Body Text** | MYRIAD PRO / Montserrat / Webly Sleek (clean, geometric sans) |
| **Accent / Callouts** | Custom outlined, stencil, or stretched display fonts (used sparingly) |

---

## Layout Style

- Heavy outline frames, bold dividers
- Grid-based panels and icon slots
- Balance of text blocks and large-scale icons
- Structured but energetic

---

## Iconography & Illustration

### Style
- Monoline or filled black/white icons
- Sticker-style, playful with meaning
- Outline stroke thickness consistent: **2pt–3pt on print scale**

### Common Icons
| Icon | Symbol |
|---|---|
| Lightning Bolt | ⚡ |
| Globe | 🌎 |
| Lab Flask | 🧪 |
| Spark / Starburst | ✨ |
| Paint tools / Tag shapes | 🎨 |
| Apparel symbols | 👕 (T-shirt, hangtag, barcode) |

### Usage Rules
- Layered in posters and packaging
- Icons sit in grid modules
- Backgrounds feature halftones, dot noise, or riso textures

---

## Packaging & Display Elements

### Mystery Box System
- Main phrase: **"What's in the Box?!"**
- Uses halftone texture + bold outlines + expressive icons
- Color combos: Blue/black/white OR red/yellow/black
- Aesthetic: Retro ad energy meets streetwear drop

### Sticker Pack Visuals
- Icon mashups, humor-first, mix editorial and merch
- Small caption text: "Certified Creative Mischief" / "Open at your own risk"

### Display Copy Callouts
- ✨ Art you can wear
- 🎁 Unbox something extraordinary
- ☕ Chaos. Creativity. Coffee.
- 👀 Contents may cause extreme coolness

---

## Product Line Language

### Apparel Labels
- "The Product Lab"
- "Experimental Goods Unit"
- "Lab Tested. Street Approved."
- "Crafted by Ink Fish"

### Category Icons
- Keychains, Pins, Tote Bags, T-Shirts, Bottle Art
- Minimalist illustrated icon per product

### T-Shirt Label Grid Format
- Blocked layouts like vintage boarding passes
- Globe / Barcode / Flash / Flask icons
- 1 or 2 color only (usually black on off-white)

## Social / Content Systems

### Instagram Style
- Poster-style campaign visuals
- AI-enhanced hero graphics
- Tear tab layouts ("Pick Your Power-Up!")
- Carousel posts that stack content like zines

### Language & Copywriting Style
- ⚡ Action verbs: "Pick. Grab. Scan. Tag."
- 🤘 Tone: expressive, witty, unfiltered
- 💬 Use of short bold headers and playful emojis

### Campaign Archetypes
| Campaign Type | Phrase / Hook |
|---|---|
| Internships | "Pick Your Power-Up!" |
| Product Drops | "What's in the Box?!" |
| Display Banners | "Tag It Your Way" / "Ear-resistible Energy" |

## Brand Systems & Assets

### Creative Architecture
- Logo Block (centered or left-aligned)
- CTA blocks (framed "Apply now", "Scan me", etc.)
- Modular Frames with icons & slogans
- Rounded border layouts for packaging and labels

### Templates in Use

| Ratio | Format |
|---|---|
| 5:2 | Label tag cards |
| 3:4 | Posters |
| 1:1 | Carousels |
| 4:6 | Retail signage |

### Brand Copy Phrases (Master List)
- Lab Tested. Street Approved.
- Crafted by Ink Fish
- Experimental Goods Only
- Designed to Disrupt
- Made for Merch Lovers

## Design System Checklist ✅

- [ ] All borders clean and evenly spaced?
- [ ] CMYK tone in every set?
- [ ] Icons style-matched and aligned?
- [ ] Copy clear, bold, and not overused?
- [ ] Text hierarchy visible from a distance?
- [ ] All output production-friendly?
- [ ] Does it feel "fun + collectible"?
