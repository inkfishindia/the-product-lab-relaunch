# The Product Lab (TPL) — Company Overview

## Core Identity

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Hybrid print-on-demand (PoD) + omnichannel retail company |
| **Parent Venture** | Ink Fish |
| **Mission** | "Empower creators and brands through friction-free design-to-product experiences." |
| **Alt Mission (Creative Guide)** | "Empower creators and fans with expressive, collectible, and creatively experimental merchandise." |
| **Differentiator** | Integrates online PoD marketplace, B2B manufacturing, micro-retail kiosks, and flagship experience hubs |
| **Personality** | Creative, community-first, tech-enabled, rooted in design innovation |
| **Positioning** | Artist-powered merch, made to spark joy, bold expression, and street-level curiosity |

## What TPL Does (Operational Definition)

A design-led merchandise & community platform that partners with artists and brands to create, produce, and distribute culturally relevant, high-quality products.

## How TPL Operates

- Print-on-demand & hybrid inventory
- Artist-led designs, B2B & B2C retail
- Online + Offline (website, pop-ups, collabs, events)
- Vertical integration: design, production, branding, sales, logistics

---

## Business Verticals

| Vertical | Description |
|---|---|
| **Ecommerce Retail + Marketplace** | Limited edition D2C capsule collections designed by the TPL studio team + an artist-led PoD platform where creators upload designs and TPL handles everything from production to shipping |
| **Micro-Retail POS** | Modular retail kiosks in coffee shops, arcades, events |
| **B2B & White-Label Merchandising** | Merchandise production, corporate gifting, agency fulfillment & dropshipping |
| **El Patio Flagship** | A physical experience hub combining retail, workshops, creator incubation & brand activations |
| **Artist Supply Module** | Provides product merchandise for creators' own stores |
