# The Product Lab (TPL) — Operations, Team & Tools

## Team Composition (Starting / Intern Squad)

| Role | Responsibilities |
|---|---|
| **Creative** | Illustration, graphic design, mockups, branding assets, video, photography |
| **Marketing & Sales** | Outreach, brand decks, artist onboarding, research |
| **Campaign / Strategy Intern (1)** | Campaign calendar, launches, GTM planning, IG Story & Reels, UGC |
| **Sprint Manager** | Project planning, weekly check-ins, reviews, tracking, Airtable management, operations support |

---

## Tools & Systems

| Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Notion or Airtable** | Project tracking, sprint planning, task logging |
| **Figma / GDrive / Canva** | Design & asset hub |
| **Slack or WhatsApp** | Daily updates & feedback loops |
| **Google Meet** | Weekly squad syncs & reviews |
| **Drop Folder (Drive)** | All final project outputs go here |

---

## Operational Model

| Dimension | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Inventory Model** | Print-on-demand + hybrid inventory |
| **Design Model** | Artist-led designs + in-house studio team |
| **Sales Channels** | B2B + B2C |
| **Distribution** | Online (ecommerce) + Offline (pop-ups, collabs, events, kiosks, flagship) |
| **Integration** | Vertical: design → production → branding → sales → logistics |

---

## Production Infrastructure

| Component | Detail |
|---|---|
| **TPL HQ** | Assembly and dispatch center |
| **Vendor Network** | Multi-factory ecosystem (external) |
| **In-House Support** | Quality control, design adaptation, packaging |
| **ERP Integration** | Planned: Sync ERP with Airtable for live production tracking and auto-order processing |

---

## Workflow Notes

- Each vertical operates as a full business stream with supporting departments
- Sprint-based project management using Airtable
- Weekly review cadence via Google Meet
- All final outputs stored in designated Google Drive Drop Folder
- Daily communication via Slack or WhatsApp
