## Policy Pages (Rewritten for TPL)

In a conversation dated September 2025, Dan provided YDS policy page URLs for rewriting as TPL policies:

### Source URLs (YDS Original)
- Privacy Policy: `https://www.yourdesignstore.in/pages/privacypolicy`
- Return & Refunds: `https://www.yourdesignstore.in/pages/returnandrefunds`
- Shipping Policy: `https://www.yourdesignstore.in/pages/shippingpolicy`
- Cancellation Policy: `https://www.yourdesignstore.in/pages/cancellationpolicy`

### Rewrite Approach
All four policies were rewritten for The Product Lab with:
- **Creator-first, non-corporate language** (aligned with TPL brand voice)
- **Structure:** Benefits before restrictions
- **Transparency:** Clear about TPL's role and support
- **Community:** Policies reinforce creative community values
- **Trust:** Emphasis on protecting creator interests

### Information Still Needed From Dan
1. **Legal Details:** Registered business name, address, GST number
2. **Payment Processing:** Payment gateway provider, accepted methods
3. **Operational Details:** Shipping partners, actual shipping costs/zones, international shipping (Y/N), production timelines by category
4. **Legal Requirements:** GST/PAN requirements, data retention period, dispute resolution process, jurisdiction details

### Testing Recommendations
- A/B test policy page engagement (creator-friendly vs traditional legal language)
- Monitor support ticket reduction post-implementation
- Track creator onboarding completion rates
- Measure customer trust scores
