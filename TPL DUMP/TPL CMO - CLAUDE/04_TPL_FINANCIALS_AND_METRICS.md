## KPI Framework

### Cultural Transformation Metrics
1. **Family Validation Rate** — % of creators reporting improved family support
2. **Creator Career Confidence** — Monthly survey score (1-10)
3. **Community Advocacy Growth** — Creator referral and testimonial rates
4. **Cultural Authority Recognition** — Media mentions and industry recognition

### Operational Excellence Metrics
1. **Sprint Goal Achievement** — % of weekly commitments completed
2. **Cultural Intelligence Integration** — % of insights that influence strategy
3. **Team Capacity Optimization** — Productive hours vs planned hours
4. **Strategic Milestone Progress** — % completion against quarterly goals

### Weekly Sprint Success Metrics (Template)
- Revenue: ₹40,000
- Community Growth: 50 new followers/community members
- Content Engagement: 7% average engagement rate
- Creator Interviews: 5 completed with insights documented

### Leading Indicators
1. Cultural conversation volume (creator discussions about family validation)
2. Content engagement depth (time spent with family-focused content)
3. Community health score (creator satisfaction + participation rates)
4. External recognition growth (industry + media attention trends)
