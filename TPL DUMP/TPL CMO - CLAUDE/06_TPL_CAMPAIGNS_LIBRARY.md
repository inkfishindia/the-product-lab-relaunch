# 06 — TPL Campaigns Library

## Campaign 1: "Card Identity" / "Card Statement"

### Overview
| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Product Focus** | Credit/Debit Card Stickers (Skins) |
| **Target Audience** | 18-24 (extending to 30); college kids, expressive people, art fans |
| **Channels** | Instagram, WhatsApp, Website |
| **Campaign Name (v1)** | "Card Identity: Transform Everyday Finance into Personal Expression" |
| **Campaign Name (v2 — Refined)** | "Card Statement: What Does Your Card Say When You Pay?" |

### Strategic Positioning
- **v1 Frame:** "Banking Rebellion" / "Card Identity Movement" — subverting corporate banking aesthetics
- **v2 Frame (Selected):** "What does your card say about you?" / "What statement are you making when you pull out your card?" — personal expression, not rebellion
- Purple Cow factor: payment moments as micro-opportunities for personality expression

### Key Messages
- "Your wallet tells your story"
- "Why let banks define your aesthetic?"
- "Easily identify your cards while expressing your style"

### Phase Structure
| Phase | Duration | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| DISCOVER | 2 weeks | Cultural research, artist collaboration (3-5 Bangalore artists), street interviews, influencer seeding |
| DEFINE | 1 week | Positioning, messaging framework |
| DESIGN | 1 week | Content creation, experience architecture |
| DEPLOY | 6 weeks | Testing, optimization, scaling |

### Implementation Timeline
- **Week 1-2:** Preparation — finalize 10 statement designs, recruit campus ambassadors (5 Bangalore colleges), content templates, analytics setup
- **Week 3:** Soft Launch — ambassador seeding, reaction content collection, focus groups, website quiz
- **Week 4:** Full Launch — main collection release (Rich AF, Just Buy It), Instagram content series, WhatsApp broadcast, campus activation
- **Weeks 5-10:** Amplification — statement submission contest (W5-6), campus designs (W7), user-generated designs (W9), optimization (ongoing)

### Content Series
- "Card Reactions" — genuine reactions at payment
- "Card Personality Types" — designs matched to personalities
- "Statement of the Week" — best-selling/creative designs
- "College Card Culture" — campus adoption highlights

### Risks & Mitigations
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Bank policy on covering logos | Clear guidelines on safe coverage areas |
| Application quality issues | Video tutorials, replacement guarantee, proprietary application tool |
| Copyright on custom designs | TOS for uploads, review process, legal guidelines |
| Slow initial adoption | Free samples for influencers, in-store demos, money-back guarantee (first 100) |
| Limited repeat purchases | Collectible drops, seasonal collections (festivals, exams, graduation) |

## Production Efficiency Guidelines (All Campaigns)

1. **Batched Production:** Dedicated days for shooting multiple pieces
2. **Template Utilization:** Reusable templates per content series
3. **Content Flexibility:** 70% planned, 30% reactive
4. **Performance-Based Adaptation:** Weekly adjustments from engagement data
5. **Community Amplification:** UGC reduces production burden
6. **Style Consistency:** Visual cohesion within each archetype
7. **Cross-Platform Efficiency:** Create once, adapt many times
