## Detailed Product Breakdown

### Accessories

#### Keychains
- **Types:** Metal, custom-designed, themed collections
- **Design Examples:** "Bullshit Remover", "Suck It", "Monday Morning", "Always Tired", "Take Me Out"
- **Marketing Angle:** Low-commitment, high-impact identity expression; conversation starters
- **Placement Ideas:** Bags, jackets, hats, cork boards, car dashboards

#### Fridge Magnets
- **Types:** Themed, custom, collectible
- **Marketing Angle:** Personal expression, gifting culture, collectible mentality
- **Placement Ideas:** Fridges, whiteboards, metal surfaces

#### Lapel Pins
- **Features:** Various clasp mechanisms, size options, material quality
- **Marketing Angle:** Professional/creative identity signals
- **Placement Ideas:** Bags, jackets, hats, cork boards

#### Card Stickers (Credit/Debit Card Skins)
- **Options:** Half coverage and Full coverage
- **Customization:** Pre-designed options on website + custom design uploads
- **Design Examples:**
  - "Monopoly" themed
  - "Rich AF"
  - "Just Buy It"
  - "Hang On Let Me Think About This"
  - Monopoly character "Chance" — "Advance to the bar on your way home, tap to redeem your drink"
  - "That's All Folks" design replica
- **Key Features:** Easy to apply and change, durable, fun
- **Design Categories:** Humorous, Sarcastic, Inspirational, Flex
- **Marketing Angle:** Payment moments as micro-opportunities for self-expression

#### 3D Stickers
- **Features:** Dimension details, specific application methods, durability
- **Placement Ideas:** Water bottles, bicycle helmets, instrument cases, laptops

#### Laptop Stickers
- **Marketing Angle:** Creative expression, brand identity, workspace personalization

## Expression Archetypes (Marketing Segmentation)

Products are mapped not just by category but by **expression personality types**:

| Archetype | Description | Example Products |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| **The Witty Rebel** | Sarcastic, edgy, irreverent | "Bullshit Remover" keychain, "Suck It" keychain |
| **The Pop Culture Devotee** | Fan items, fandom loyalty | Marvel keychain, Harry Potter items, anime products |
| **The Aesthetic Minimalist** | Clean, subtle, artistic | Minimal design notebooks, subtle statement pieces |
| **The Bold Maximalist** | Colorful, loud, statement | Bright statement keychains, bold coasters |
| **The Nostalgic Soul** | Retro, childhood, vintage | Vintage-themed magnets, retro design stickers |
| **The Digital Native** | Tech references, memes, internet culture | Meme stickers, tech-reference keychains |

### Content Shift Decision
> **Decision:** Reduce product-focused content by 60%, increase expression-focused storytelling by 200%. Show how products enable identity expression, not just what products are.

## Card Skins — Deep Dive (Dedicated Campaign Product)

### Specifications
- **Coverage Options:** Half skin, Full skin
- **Application:** Easy to apply, easy to change
- **Compatibility:** Credit cards, Debit cards
- **Customization:** Choose from website designs OR upload custom designs

### Design Catalog (Confirmed)
- "Rich AF"
- "Just Buy It"
- "Hang On Let Me Think About This"
- Monopoly-themed designs
- Monopoly character "Chance" card design
- "That's All Folks" design replica

### Retail Strategy
- Design organization: By mood/statement type (Humorous, Sarcastic, Inspirational, Flex)
- Bundle options: "Statement Wardrobe" (multiple designs at discount)
- Seasonal collections: Festivals, exam season, graduation
- College-specific designs for campus rivalry campaigns
