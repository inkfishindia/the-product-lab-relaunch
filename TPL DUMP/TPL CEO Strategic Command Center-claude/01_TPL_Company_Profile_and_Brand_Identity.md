## BUSINESS MODEL

### Core Positioning
- **Model:** Hybrid — Marketplace + Agency for artist-led merchandise
- **Differentiation:** Artist-first platform with JIT (Just-In-Time) production
- **Competitive Moat:** Ink Fish production infrastructure (11-year legacy) + artist ecosystem + no-MOQ flexibility
- **Architecture Philosophy:**
  - Systematic Excellence (Jeff Bezos) — every process documented and optimized
  - Operational Flow (Toyota) — lean principles with waste elimination
  - Data-Driven Decisions (Amazon) — real-time metrics driving strategy
  - Creative Authenticity (TPL) — maintaining artistic integrity while scaling
