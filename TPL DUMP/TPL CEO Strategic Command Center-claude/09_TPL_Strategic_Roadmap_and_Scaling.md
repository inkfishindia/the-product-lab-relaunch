## RISK MANAGEMENT FRAMEWORK

### Operational Risks
| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|
| Production capacity constraints (peak seasons Q4/Q1) | High | Ink Fish capacity modeling, scaling agreements |
| Quality consistency at scale | High | Statistical process control, predictive monitoring |
| Ink Fish shared facility competing priorities | Medium | Dedicated TPL production zones, clear SLAs |
| Artist dependency (4 artists = concentration risk) | Medium | Expand to 8+ artists, diversify styles |
| Single point of failure (solo operator) | Critical | Hire ops team, automate, document everything |

### Financial Risks
| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|
| Cash flow during automation investment | High | Stage investments with revenue milestones |
| B2B longer sales cycles impacting cash flow | Medium | Bridge financing, credit facilities |
| Enterprise client concentration | Medium | Portfolio diversification across company sizes |
| Inventory overstock on experimental designs | Medium | ABC analysis, market validation before production |

### Strategic Risks
| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|
| Competitor replication of model | Medium | Build moats faster (artist network, ops excellence) |
| Market saturation in creative commerce | Low | Artist-first positioning is defensible |
| Technology obsolescence | Low | Modular architecture, platform-agnostic data |
| Regulatory changes (e-commerce, tax) | Low | Legal compliance framework, advisory |

### Implementation Risks
| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|
| Dan's consistency weakness | High | Structure over perfection, automated reminders, accountability systems |
| Team adoption resistance | Medium | Phased rollout, training, quick wins first |
| Data migration errors | Medium | Parallel run, validation checks, rollback plan |
| Integration failures (WooCommerce ↔ Airtable) | Medium | Staging environment, thorough testing |
