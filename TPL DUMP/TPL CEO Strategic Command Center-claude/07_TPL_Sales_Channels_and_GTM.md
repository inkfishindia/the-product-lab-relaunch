## B2B LAUNCH PLAN

### Market Opportunity
- Indian corporate gifting market: **₹72,000 crore annually, 15% CAGR**
- Post-COVID shift toward authentic, personalized corporate merchandise
- Enterprise buyers value creative differentiation over cost optimization
- Artist-led products command **40-60% premium** in B2B

### B2B Financial Model
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Revenue potential | ₹8-12L monthly within 6 months |
| Average order value | ₹25,000-₹75,000 |
| Gross margins | 65-70% (vs 75% D2C, volume discounts) |
| Customer LTV | 5-8x higher than D2C |
| Sales cycle | 2-4 weeks |
| Investment required | ₹3-5L for sales infrastructure |

### B2B 5-Project Launch Framework

**Project 1: Enterprise Sales Foundation (30 days, ₹1.5L)**
- Competitor analysis: Corporate gifting landscape
- Tiered volume-based pricing structure
- Artist-led differentiation messaging
- Target segmentation: Tech, BFSI, FMCG verticals
- CRM enhancement: Airtable B2B pipeline
- Sales collateral: Enterprise presentation deck
- Quote automation: Pricing calculator

**Project 2: Pipeline Development (60 days, ₹1L)**
- Direct outreach: 100+ target enterprises
- Strategic partnerships: HR/Admin consulting firms, event management, corporate travel
- Industry events: Design conferences, HR summits, startup events
- Digital presence: LinkedIn thought leadership, case studies

**Project 3: Product & Service Innovation (90 days, ₹1.5L)**
- Enterprise customization: Department-specific designs
- Subscription model: Quarterly merchandise boxes
- Experience packages: Artist workshop + merchandise bundles
- White-label program: For agencies and designers
- Seasonal collections: Festival, year-end, onboarding kits

**Project 4: Operational Excellence (120 days, ₹2L)**
- Dedicated B2B production line
- Enterprise quality standards (ISO-aligned)
- Priority fulfillment workflow
- Credit management: Corporate payment terms

**Project 5: Strategic Differentiation (120 days, ₹2L)**
- Thought leadership: "Creative Commerce in Indian Enterprises" white paper
- Innovation Labs: Corporate art-thinking workshops
- Office space creative transformation services
- Employee engagement: Artist collaboration programs

### B2B Data Points
- First B2B customer in Goa: **AMB - Amoeba**
- Geographic expansion signal validated
- Q4/Q1 optimal timing for corporate budget planning cycles
