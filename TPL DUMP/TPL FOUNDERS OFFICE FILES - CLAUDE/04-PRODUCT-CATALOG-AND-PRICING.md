### Apparel Production Methods

| Method | Best For | Advantages |
|--------|----------|------------|
| DTG (Direct-to-Garment) | Photo prints, detailed designs | Unlimited colors, photo-realistic |
| DTF (Direct-to-Film) | Durable transfers, vibrant colors | Works on various fabrics, dark garments |
| Vinyl Cutting | Precise text, simple graphics | Extremely durable, professional finish |
| Screen Printing | Bulk orders, solid colors | Cost-effective at scale |
| Embroidery | Logos, small designs | Premium finish |
| Heat Press | Quick turnaround, simple apps | Fast |
| Sublimation | Full-coverage prints | All-over prints, polyester garments |

## PRODUCT PERFORMANCE FRAMEWORK (BCG Matrix)

| Category | Products | Strategy |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Stars** (High Growth, High Share) | Custom t-shirts, artist keychains, limited edition bags | Maintain and expand |
| **Cash Cows** (Low Growth, High Share) | Standard notebooks, basic accessories, classic designs | Maximize margins |
| **Question Marks** (High Growth, Low Share) | Home decor, premium stationery, digital-physical hybrids | Invest or divest |
| **Dogs** (Low Growth, Low Share) | Underperforming designs, seasonal mismatches | Phase out or reposition |
