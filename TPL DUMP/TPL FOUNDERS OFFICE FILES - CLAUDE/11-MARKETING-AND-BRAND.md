## Brand Identity System (from Creative Direction conversation)

### Creative Positioning
- **Concept**: "Collectible Everyday" — transforming mundane items into cultural significance
- **Identity**: Design curator and cultural amplifier
- **Principles**: Bold Simplicity, Cultural Synthesis, Functional Expression, Collector's Mindset

### Typography System (Discussed/Recommended)

**Current Approach**: Futura MD BT primary + Webly Sleek + Dancing Script

**Recommended Evolution** (from Creative Director session):

| Role | Current | Recommended Direction |
|------|---------|----------------------|
| Primary (Headlines) | Futura MD BT | More distinctive: Neue Haas Grotesk, GT America, or Pangram Sans |
| Secondary (Editorial) | Webly Sleek | Refined serif like Canela for artist features |
| Accent | Dancing Script | Orkney (geometric sans) for product names — drop Dancing Script |
| Body | Futura | Aktiv Grotesk Regular |

📌 DECISION PENDING: Dan acknowledged the recommendations but final typography selection not confirmed.

## Marketing Performance (Current vs Target)

| ROAS | 0.78x | 4-5x |
| Conversion Rate | 0.63% | 2-3% |

### Recommended Fixes (from CEO session)
1. Full-funnel website analysis for drop-off points
2. A/B testing framework for product pages and checkout
3. Mobile experience optimization (>70% traffic likely mobile)
4. Social proof and urgency elements at checkout
5. Retargeting campaigns for cart abandonment
6. Lookalike audiences from best customers
7. Strategic product bundling to increase AOV
8. Free shipping threshold optimization (₹499 → ₹699)
