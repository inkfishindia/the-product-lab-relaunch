## Room 1: INFLOW/OUTFLOW CONTROL (Small)
**Primary Assignment**: Muskan's Operations Hub

### Equipment & Setup
- Desk with computer/tablet for Airtable access
- Printer for order documentation and job cards
- Phone system for client communication

- Client meetings and order confirmations

## Room 2: INVENTORY MANAGEMENT CENTER (Really Small)
**Primary Assignment**: Kalpana (Inventory Master)

### Rack System

**Rack 1: PENDING PROCESSING (5 Shelves)**
- Shelf 1: New factory deliveries awaiting sort
- Shelf 2: Parts and accessories needing assembly
- Shelf 3: Quality control hold items
- Shelf 4: Incomplete orders awaiting components
- Shelf 5: Rework and revision items

**Rack 2: READY STOCK (5 Shelves)**
- Shelf 1: Accessories (keychains, magnets, lapel pins)
- Shelf 2: Stationery (stickers, notebooks, bookmarks)
- Shelf 3: Home & Decor (coasters, clocks, music plaques)
- Shelf 4: Apparel items (folded and ready)
- Shelf 5: Packaging materials and assembly components

### Equipment
- Central work table (1.2m × 0.8m) for sorting
- 6 mobile sorting bins for category separation
- Barcode scanner for inventory tracking
- Tablet/laptop for Airtable updates
- Label printer for shelf organization
- Clear storage containers for small parts
- Hand cart for moving inventory between rooms

## Material Flow Through Rooms

```
Factory Deliveries → Room 1 (Log & Tray)
  → Room 2 (Sort & Inventory)
    → Room 4 (Assembly & QC)
      → Room 3 (Pack into Orders)
        → Room 5 (Dispatch)
          → Customer
```
