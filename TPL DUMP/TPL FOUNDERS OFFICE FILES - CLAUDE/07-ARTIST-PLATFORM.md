## Artist Tier System 🔢

| Tier | Classification | Revenue Threshold | Revenue Share | Support Level | Products/Quarter |
|------|---------------|-------------------|--------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Tier 1 | Rising Stars | <₹25k/month | 40% of margin | Basic tools, templates | 5-10 designs |
| Tier 2 | Established Artists | ₹25k–₹1L/month | 50% of margin | Advanced analytics, marketing | 15-25 designs |
| Tier 3 | Cultural Icons | >₹1L/month | 60% of margin | Full-service partnership | Signature collections |

### Tier Advancement Criteria
- Consistent sales performance
- Professional reliability
- Creative growth and evolution
- Brand value alignment
- Formal review at 6 months; tier assessment at 12 months
- Target advancement rate: 25% annually

## Revenue Share Calculation Formula 🔢

### Example: ₹1,000 Item

| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Base Cost (direct material/production) | ₹250 |
| Customization (design-specific) | ₹100 |
| Lab Fee (20% — platform maintenance, operations) | ₹200 |
| **Net Margin** | **₹450** |
| Artist Share (40-60% of margin based on tier) | ₹180–₹270 |
| Platform Share (remaining margin) | ₹180–₹270 |

### Example: ₹199 Keychain (Tier 1 Artist)

| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Base Material Cost | ₹45 |
| Production Labor | ₹20 |
| Customization Cost | ₹15 |
| Packaging | ₹10 |
| **Total Direct Cost** | **₹90** |
| Lab Fee (20%) | ₹40 |
| **Total Cost + Fees** | **₹130** |
| **Net Margin** | **₹69** |
| Artist Share (40% of margin) | ₹27.60 |
| Platform Share (60% of margin) | ₹41.40 |

### At 50 Units/Month
- Total Revenue: ₹9,950
- Total Artist Payment: ₹1,380
- Platform Revenue: ₹2,070
- COGS: ₹6,500
- Gross Margin Rate: 35%
- Artist Share of Retail: 14%
- Platform Share of Retail: 21%

## Artist Onboarding Schedule (10-Week Process) 🔢

| Week | Activity | Deliverables | Responsible |
|------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | Contract finalization | Signed agreement | Community Manager |
| 1 | Platform introduction | Account setup, systems training | Operations Assistant |
| 2 | Creative briefing | Style guide, product direction | Creative Director |
| 2 | Technical specifications | File templates, production guidelines | 2D Graphic Designer |
| 3 | Initial concept development | First design drafts (5-10 concepts) | Artist |
| 4 | Design review and feedback | Revision notes, narrow to 3-5 | Creative Director |
| 5 | Final design approval | Production-ready files | Creative Director/Artist |
| 6 | Production planning | Timeline, quantities, materials | Operations |
| 7-8 | Production and QC | Finished products | Production Team |
| 9 | Launch planning | Marketing assets, drop date | Marketing Team |
| 10 | Drop execution | Live sale | All Team |

## Contract Essentials Checklist

- [ ] Revenue share percentage stated
- [ ] Term length (typically 12 months)
- [ ] Payment schedule (monthly cycle, statement by 5th, payment by 10th)
- [ ] IP rights clarified
- [ ] Marketing authorization
- [ ] Exclusivity provisions (if any)
- [ ] Termination conditions
- [ ] Dispute resolution
- [ ] Confidentiality requirements

## Payment Processing 🔢

- Monthly payment cycle
- Statement generation by 5th of month
- Payment processing by 10th of month
- Preferred method: Direct bank transfer
- Records maintained for accounting and tax
