## Order-to-Fulfillment Workflow

```
1. Order Received → Automated system capture
2. Design Validation → File check and optimization
3. Production Queue → Assigned to appropriate factory partner
4. Production → Printing/engraving based on method
5. Quality Control → Inspection at factory facility
6. Transfer to Ink Fish → Final assembly and packaging
7. Packaging → Brand-standard packaging
8. Dispatch → Ship within 24-48 hours
```

## Quality Control Points

1. Design files: Resolution, color, size verification
2. Material quality: Consistency check on raw materials
3. Production process: Monitoring during printing/engraving
4. Finished product: Final inspection before assembly
5. Packaging: Proper protection and presentation
6. Pre-dispatch: Final verification against order

## POD/JIT System Advantages

1. Zero inventory risk — no capital tied up in stock
2. Infinite customization — every order can be unique
3. Rapid scaling — production scales with demand
4. Quality freshness — everything made fresh per order
5. Cost efficiency — no warehousing costs
6. Sustainability — zero waste from unsold inventory
7. Market responsiveness — adapt to trends immediately
