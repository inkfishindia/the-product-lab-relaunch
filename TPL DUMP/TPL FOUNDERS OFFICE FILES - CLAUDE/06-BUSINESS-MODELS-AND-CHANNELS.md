## Model 2: Business-to-Business (B2B)

### Client Portfolio 🔢
Meta, Diageo, Coca-Cola, Amazon (via Ink Fish)

### B2B Wholesale Discount Structure

| Product Category | Standard Discount | Margin Retention |
|-----------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Accessories | 40% off retail | 60% of MRP |
| Apparel | 35% off retail | 65% of MRP |
| Stationery | 40% off retail | 60% of MRP |
| Home & Decor | 30% off retail | 70% of MRP |

### Volume Discount Tiers

| Quantity | Additional Discount | Total Discount |
|----------|---------------------|---------------|
| 10-24 pieces | Standard B2B rate | As per category |
| 25-49 pieces | +5% additional | Category + 5% |
| 50-99 pieces | +10% additional | Category + 10% |
| 100+ pieces | +15% additional | Category + 15% |

### Custom Project Pricing Formula 🔢

```
Base Price = (Material Costs + Production Hours × Labor Rate + Design Hours × Design Rate) × (1 + Overhead %) × (1 + Profit Margin %)
```
- Labor Rate: ₹250-350/hour
- Custom branding: +15% of order value
- Rush orders: +20% premium
- Exclusive designs: +25% premium
- Minimum order: ₹25,000

### Payment Terms
- Established partners: Net 30-45
- New clients: COD
- Large orders: 50% advance, balance on delivery
