## Experience Center Concept (from Interior Design session)

### Design Philosophy
"The Product Lab Experience Center" — hybrid retail showcase, artist platform, creative workshop, brand storytelling destination. Combines industrial workshop aesthetics with contemporary gallery elements.

### Key Design Elements

**1. Modular Retail System**
- Industrial pipe frameworks with custom wooden platforms
- Movable display modules on casters for event reconfiguration
- Colorful acrylic product risers and magnetic wall panels
- Interactive "touch and feel" stations

**2. Artist Showcase Zones**
- Dedicated gallery walls with integrated lighting
- Suspended hanging system for 2D artwork and textiles
- Biography pods with digital screens for artist stories
- Illuminated niches for signature pieces

**3. Workshop & Customization Bar**
- Central workspace with durable countertops and visible tool organization
- Live customization station (customers watch personalization)
- Demonstration area with tiered seating for artist talks
- Mobile equipment carts

**4. Production Visibility**
- Semi-transparent glass partition revealing production
- Exposed material library
- Digital production queue display
- Framed viewing windows ("production as performance")

### Sensory Experience Design
- **Visual**: Gallery-like lighting with focused product highlights
- **Audio**: Curated brand playlist
- **Scent**: Subtle signature scent (wood/paper notes)
- **Touch**: Tactile material samples and interactive displays
- **Taste**: Partnership with local beverage provider for events
