## Vision, Mission & BHAG

**Mission Statement**: To build India's most dynamic creative lab by empowering artists, co-creating with brands, and making drop culture accessible to everyday life.

**Vision Statement**: To become India's first independent design-label platform — part marketplace, part agency, part cultural archive — producing art-led products, incubating creative talent, and influencing the visual language of Indian youth culture.

**BHAG**: To become India's most influential independent design lab — building a creative ecosystem where artists, brands, and culture collide — and turning everyday products into collectible expressions of identity, powered by community.

## Core Business Model (Hybrid)

1. **Creative Marketplace** — Platform for artists to monetize their designs
2. **Direct-to-Consumer Brand** — Producing unique, limited-edition products
3. **B2B Service Provider** — Creating custom merchandise for corporate clients
4. **Cultural Curator** — Influencing youth culture through design and collaborations
