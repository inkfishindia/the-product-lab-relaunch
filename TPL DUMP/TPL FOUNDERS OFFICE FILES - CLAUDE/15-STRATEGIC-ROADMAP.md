## 90-Day Revenue Optimization & Systems Plan

### Track 1: Revenue Engine Optimization (Weeks 1-6)

**D2C Conversion Optimization**
1. Full-funnel website analysis for drop-off points
2. A/B testing framework for product pages and checkout
3. Mobile experience optimization
4. Social proof, urgency elements, friction reduction at checkout

**ROAS Improvement**
1. Audit current ad campaigns
2. Implement proper tracking and attribution
3. Build retargeting campaigns for cart abandonment
4. Develop lookalike audiences from best customers

**AOV Enhancement**
1. Strategic product bundling (accessories + lifestyle items)
2. Free shipping threshold optimization (₹499 → ₹699)
3. Complementary product recommendations
4. Tiered pricing structure across categories

**B2B Sales Systematization**
1. Document B2B sales process from lead to fulfillment
2. Standardized proposal templates for corporate clients
3. Corporate client dashboard in Airtable
