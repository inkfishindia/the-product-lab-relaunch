# Seeding Kit Creative Brief

> Package design and messaging for the 30-product gifting program (D-007, D-013).

## Purpose

Send 30+ products to Design Yatra contacts, journalists, micro-influencers, and key community members before launch. This is social proof — not advertising.

## Package Contents (per kit)

- 2–3 TPL products (gift from Rachel's list)
- 1× handwritten-style note card (printed is fine)
- 1× small sticker sheet (TPL brand)
- Optional: 1× "you're getting this early" card

## Note Card Copy

> Written per Joanna's copy-system voice rules.

**Version A (Direct):**
```
You're getting this before anyone else.
The Product Lab relaunches [DATE].
Wear your opinion.

— Dan, TPL
theproductlab.in
```

**Version B (Warmer):**
```
This is our new work.
We made it for people with opinions.
Thought of you.

Drop goes live [DATE] at theproductlab.in
```

## Packaging Visual Direction

- Outer: Kraft box or black mailer bag (no white boxes)
- TPL logo stamp: Black or white, depending on surface
- Tape: Black kraft tape (no branded tape needed)
- Insert card: Off-white (#F5F0EB equivalent), Barlow Condensed print

## Tracking

Rachel manages the list. Each kit gets a tracking note in:
`artifacts/phase-5/seeding-plan.md`

## Timeline

- Kits shipped: 2 weeks before launch date
- Follow-up DM: 1 week before launch
- Tag tracking: Monitor @theproductlab mentions
