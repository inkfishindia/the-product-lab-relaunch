## Revenue Share Model

| Tier | Revenue Share | Criteria |
|------|-------------|----------|
| New (0-3 months) | 40% of net revenue | Onboarding phase |
| Proven (3-12 months) | 50% of net revenue | Consistent performance |
| Star (12+ months) | 60% of net revenue | Track record |
| Exclusive | +10% on applicable tier | Exclusivity agreement |
| **Lab Fee** | 15-25% of final price | Applied across all tiers |

### Net Revenue Calculation

```
Gross Sales = Quantity × Selling Price
Platform Fees = Sales × Platform Fee %
Processing Fees = Sales × Payment Processing %
Net Revenue = Gross Sales - Platform Fees - Processing Fees
Artist Share = Net Revenue × Tier Percentage
```

## Onboarding (4-Week Pipeline)

- Week 1: Discovery & outreach (portfolio review, market potential, brand alignment)
- Week 2: Evaluation (scoring 7+/10 for acceptance; weights: Artistic 40%, Market 30%, Production 20%, Brand 10%)
- Week 3: Contracting & orientation (agreement, IP rights, exclusivity terms, brand guidelines)
- Week 4: First drop planning (concept → max 3 iteration rounds → final → production → QC)
