## Critical New Information (Not in Phase 1-2 Artifacts)

### 1. Actual Revenue: ₹48L Annual (₹4L/month)
Phase 1 Patrick used "₹0-40L declared" from IndiaMart. Handoff reveals the consistently cited figure is ₹48L annually. Accounts Wizard actual data shows ₹12-14L (likely partial year). This means order volume is 3-5x higher than Patrick's low-scenario estimate.

### 2. Revenue Mix Reality: 80% B2B / 20% D2C
The Master Framework claimed 75% D2C / 25% B2B. Accounts Wizard actual data shows the inverse: 80% B2B2C (wholesale) / 20% B2C. The D2C relaunch is building the minority revenue channel.

### 3. In-House Production (Ink Fish)
TPL has in-house production capability via parent company Ink Fish. Capabilities: 12-color screen printing, UV printing, digital printing, DTG, sublimation, embroidery, vinyl, DTF. Processes ~40 orders/day, 150-250 pieces. This is NOT external sourcing — it's a shared facility.

### 4. Detailed Cost Calculator
Handoff includes actual material costs, not estimates:
- Acrylic (3mm): ₹90/sq ft
- Birch: ₹43/sq ft
- MDF: ₹30/sq ft
- UV Print A4: ₹200/sheet
- Rubber pin back: ₹6/piece
- Magnetic pin back: ₹15/piece
- Labor: ₹3/piece
- Example: 1"×1" acrylic lapel pin, UV, rubber back, plain card = ₹18.33/unit

### 5. Product Categories Beyond Accessories
The handoff shows apparel (T-shirts ₹999-1,499, Hoodies ₹999-1,499, Caps, Totes) and home/decor (coasters, mugs, clocks, plaques). Phase 1-2 work focused exclusively on small accessories.

### 6. Enterprise B2B Clients
Meta, Diageo, Coca-Cola, Amazon — all corporate merchandise clients. PR Kits ₹808 AOV. Service category: ₹12K AOV (only 12 units sold but highest per-unit value).

### 7. Artist Platform Details
- 4 active artists currently
- 12+ in pipeline
- Revenue share: 40% (new) → 50% (proven) → 60% (star) + 10% exclusivity bonus
- 15-25% lab fee on all tiers
- 4-week onboarding pipeline
- Target: 8-12 active contributors

### 8. YDS Merger Negotiations (Status Unknown)
Multiple rounds of negotiation for TPL-YDS equity swap/merger:
- YDS: ₹4-4.89 Cr revenue, ₹12-14.67 Cr valuation
- TPL: ₹32L-₹1Cr revenue (disputed), ₹96L-₹3Cr valuation
- Deal involves Dan potentially becoming CCO at YDS
- STATUS UNKNOWN — needs Dan confirmation

### 9. 30+ Airtable Bases
TPL has an extensive Airtable ecosystem tracking products, inventory, orders, production, artists, pricing, and more. All of this is WooCommerce-linked and would need re-evaluation for Fynd Commerce migration.

### 10. Design Yatra 2025 Event
Sept 4-6, 2025 at Taj Cidade de Goa. Budget: ₹8.62L total (₹6.12L net after product sponsorship). 1100+ welcome kits, 800+ custom prints planned. Outcome not captured.
